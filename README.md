# Aequitas Intelligence
**High-Fidelity On-Chain Financial Architecture & Risk Simulation**

Aequitas Intelligence is a technical terminal designed to normalize **100,000 decentralized transaction logs** into structured executive insights. It utilizes deterministic math and discrete-event modeling to bridge the gap between protocol "utility" and "financial residency".

---

## 📈 Aequitas Flux: Revenue Attribution
Flux isolates the mathematical drivers behind revenue fluctuations:
* **Price Impact**: Analysis of fee-tier yields and realized yield-per-swap.
* **Volume Impact**: Isolation of raw turnover velocity and TVL utilization.
* **Mix Impact**: Identification of shifts in asset-pair composition.
* **Dual Standards**: Real-time revaluation between **IFRS** and **GAAP** reporting.

## 🚀 Aequitas Growth: Strategic GTM Model
Growth models the **"Wealth Management Pivot"** to stabilize the revenue floor:
* **4-Gate Funnel**: Tracks user progression from Discovery (Gate A) to 95% "Lock-in" maturity (Gate D).
* **Recovery Projection**: A 12-month model designed to bridge the identified **$13.3M deficit**.
* **Unit Economics**: Dynamic CAC/LTV evaluation for **Embedded Social** and **AI Intent Search**.
* **Capital Allocation**: Generates phased roadmap and monthly budget mandates.

## 🛡️ Aequitas Guard: Risk Simulation
Guard provides **Value-at-Risk (VaR)** assessments via historical stress-testing:
* **Contagion Scenarios**: Models **Stablecoin De-pegging**, **BTC Flash Crashes**, and **ETH Basis Risk**.
* **Forensic Rationale**: Links simulation depth to specific log data risk profiles.
* **Hedging Mandates**: Directs **Delta-Neutral Rebalancing** and **LSD-Spot Basis** strategies.

## ⚙️ Data Science & Logic
* **Discrete-Event Modeling**: Processes 100,000 unique user journeys with randomized noise factors.
* **Deterministic Core**: Pure Python execution for 100% mathematical ground-truth.
* **Data Rigor**: Automated **Median-Imputation** and **IQR Outlier Detection** for sparse logs.
* **Auditability**: Full-population audit tool with manual CSV download capabilities.
